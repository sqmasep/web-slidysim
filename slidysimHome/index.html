What is Slidysim?

Slidysim is the best 15 Puzzle/sliding puzzle simulator for PC. It was developed by ben1996123 (who is still active in the community), having a total of 28 versions, the current one being Slidysim 28.3, which hasn't been renewed in over a year. Here are some reasons why Slidysim tops the pack of sliding puzzle simulators. First, you can play square/rectangular puzzles of any size you wish, with options of keyboard or mouse controls to suit your liking. Secondly, there are over 20 unique and fun display types, each of different difficulty and testing different abilities. Slidysim also offers several modes, such as relays, marathons, and even blindfolded mode if you're up to the challenge! Most importantly, the community is the biggest sliding puzzle community in the world!
